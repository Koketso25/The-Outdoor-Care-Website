Gardening

Are you ready to take your garden to the next level? Look no further! Here are the three cutting-edge gardening services Outdoor Care offers to transform your outdoor space into a thriving oasis.

1.Vertical Gardening: Maximizing Space

WE make the most of your walls with trellises, arbors, or wall-mounted planters. Train vining plants like tomatoes, peas, or cucumbers to grow upwards, adding a new dimension to your garden.

2.Keyhole Gardening: Efficient Design

We create a circular bed with a narrow path leading to a central composting area. This design reduces waste, increases efficiency, and makes maintenance a breeze.

3.Classic Home Garden:

*We create a gerb garden near your kitchen for fresh flavors.
*Incorporate a rain garden to manage stormwater runoff.
*Build a trellis for climbing plants like roses or peas.
And much much more!!!
